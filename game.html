How to Play

Break Da Cycle is built for real life. The goal is forward movement at your pace. Thirty minutes is long enough to change direction, and short enough to stay consistent.

1) Create Your List

Write what you want to improve. Small counts. Simple counts.

2) Choose One

Select one item for today so you stay focused and avoid overwhelm.

3) Start the 30 Minute Cycle

Set a timer. Take action. When time ends, you succeeded for the day.

4) Reset Tomorrow

Stack small wins. Let time work for you instead of against you.
