Daily Missions

Pick one mission for your 30 minute cycle. Modify it to your pace when needed.

Beginner

  • Walk for ten minutes, then stretch
  • Read for fifteen minutes
  • Call someone you care about
  • Meditate or breathe for ten minutes
  • Write one page

Intermediate

  • Practice a skill for thirty minutes
  • Organize one space in your home
  • Do a light workout with intention
  • Cook a simple meal with focus
  • Journal about goals and next steps

Soul Level

  • Spend time in nature with no distractions
  • Have a meaningful conversation
  • Write down one fear and one dream
  • Prayer, silence, or meditation
  • Do something kind without announcing it

Create Your Own

  • What cycle am I breaking today
  • What is one small step forward I can take
  • How can I make it doable at my pace
  • How can I track the win
  • Who can I share progress with
